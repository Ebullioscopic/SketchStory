Privacy Policy

SketchStory Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

1. Data Collection

SketchStory does not collect personal data. The app does not create user accounts, does not track users, and does not upload stories, prompts, or images to external servers.

2. Offline by Design

SketchStory is designed to run offline with on-device intelligence features. Content generation and story workflows are intended to stay local to your device.

3. Images and Stories

If you use drawings or photos, those files are stored locally as part of your generated stories. You can remove generated content from within the app at any time.

4. Permissions

  • Camera: capture drawings or images for story inspiration.
  • Photos: select existing drawings or images from your library.

These permissions are used only for features you explicitly choose.

5. Third-Party Services

SketchStory does not use third-party ad tracking or remote profiling for personal content.

6. Children and Family Use

SketchStory is built for families. The app is intended for parents and guardians creating stories with or for children.

7. Data Control

You control your generated data on-device, including options to delete stories and images.

8. Policy Updates

If this policy changes, this page will be updated with a revised effective date.
